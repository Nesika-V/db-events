Where It All Began

A Journey Rooted in Scale.

DB Events & Productions was born from the journey of two passionate individuals whose story as life partners and business partners began amidst one of the biggest events India has ever witnessed — the 2010 Commonwealth Games.

In the middle of large-scale productions, relentless energy, creative chaos and unforgettable experiences, Disha Dhingra and Barani Chander discovered a shared passion — creating experiences that bring people together.

"Because for them, events were never just about stages, décor or logistics. They were about people. Emotion. Energy. Connection."

Over the years, that shared passion evolved into DB Events & Productions — a modern event company built to bring together creativity, operational excellence and deeply personal experiences.

Meet the Founders

The People Behind the Experiences

Barani Chander

The Mind Behind The Execution

With over two decades of experience in the events industry, Barani Chander brings the rare ability to see both the big picture and the smallest invisible detail — often before anyone else does.

His strength lies not just in execution, but in anticipation. The flow of guests. Movement backstage. Technical transitions. Artist timings. Audience energy. Operational risks most people never notice.

Calm under pressure Structured Detail-driven

Having worked on some of the country's most celebrated productions — the International Indian Film Academy Awards, Indian Premier League and large-scale experiential productions with Wizcraft International — Barani brings global production thinking into every DB event.

Disha & Barani

Co-founders, DB Events & Productions

Disha Dhingra

The Heart & Creative Spirit

Originally a Delhi Punjabi girl with an instinctive love for people, storytelling and aesthetics, Disha found her calling in creating experiences that feel deeply personal, emotionally unforgettable and beautifully designed.

Her journey began in communication, campaigns and brand experiences — working with organizations like McCann Erickson and HT Media — where she developed a strong understanding of visual storytelling and experiential communication.

"She carries the celebration emotionally alongside every family she works with."

But what truly sets Disha apart is how personally she becomes involved in every celebration she leads. Families often end up treating her like their own daughter — because her involvement goes far beyond coordination.
